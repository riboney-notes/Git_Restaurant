# Spaghetti and meatballs

- Ingredients:
  1. Ground beef
  2. Bread crumbs
  3. Parsley
  4. Parmesan cheese
  5. Garlic powder
  6. Egg
  7. Onion
  8. Garlic
  9. Canned tomatoes
  10. Salt
  11. Sugar
  12. Tomato paste
  13. Basil
  14. Black pepper

  - Steps:
  1. Combine beef, bread crumbs, parsley, cheese, pepper, garlic powder, and egg
  2. Form into balls
  3. Refrigeriate
  4. Saute onions and garlic
